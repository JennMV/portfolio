OVERVIEW

Chips n’ Dairy is a locally owned fast food business based in Ottawa that has been a part of the community for well over a decade. Having been around for years, their brand and logo was starting to look outdated.

CHALLENGE

I was presented with the opportunity to rebrand Chips n’ Dairy to appeal to a more modern society.

SOLUTION

For recreating the logo, I took a modern approach and went with a more simplistic look with flat shapes and two strong contrasting colours. The logo visually depicts exactly the name of the business by being half fries (chips) and half ice cream (dairy), making it easily identifiable with or without the wordmark. Yellow is associated with warmth which depicts the hot meals presented at Chips n’ Dairy and blue is associated with coolness which depicts the cold dairy products served in house. These colours are instantly connected to temperatures subconsciously by the viewer which creates an instant notion of what Chips n’ Dairy offers. Also the Chips n’ Dairy wordmark was hand crafted to reflect the food made in the restaurant, always made in house.
