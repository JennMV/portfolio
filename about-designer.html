Art Direction & Illustration

Yellow is associated with warmth which depicts the hot meals presented at Chips n’ Dairy and blue is associated with coolness which depicts the cold dairy products served in house. These colours are instantly connected to temperatures subconsciously by the viewer which creates an instant notion of what Chips n’ Dairy offers. Also the Chips n’ Dairy wordmark was hand crafted to reflect the food made in the restaurant, always made in house.
