OVERVIEW

CHEO (Children’s Hospital of Eastern Ontario) is a large children’s hospital located in Ottawa. Every fiscal period, the hospital releases an annual report containing the overviews on projects, successes, recognition and stories from the hospital that occurred the prior year.

CHALLENGE

To create the 2016-2017 Annual Report in both English and French. Had to explore different ways to place both languages. Some ways are you can have both English and French translations side by side on the same page, or split side by side on separate pages, or have one language written continuously on one the front side then have the other language on the back side but flipped over.

SOLUTION

I decided to have one side entirely in English and then have the flipped side in French to allow for better legibility and reduce the amount of content on a page as to not overwhelm the viewer’s eye. By doing this I allow for more white space to compliment the icons and images on the page.
